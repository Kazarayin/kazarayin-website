The Dancing Jinn

“The soul is not the price. It’s the door.” — Unknown

Chapters: 19
Pages Count: ~250
Word Count: ~50,000

A group of friends vanish during a weekend camping trip. Months later, a family searching for a fresh start moves into the same cursed forest. Years later, a struggling author follows their stories into a nightmare older than time.

Three stories. One horror. The deeper they go, the less human they become.

The Dancing Jinn is the haunting first volume in a saga of ancient magic, forgotten languages, and haunted bloodlines.

Written first in Arabic, then translated by the author, this dark fantasy tale pulls you between worlds — and never quite lets you go.
