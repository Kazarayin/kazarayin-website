Between Two Worlds

“The soul is not the price. It’s the door.”

Acts: 5.
Chapters: 99.
Pages Count: 500.
Words count: 120,000.

Synopsis:

A group of friends on a weekend camping trip vanish without a trace. Months later, a hopeful family moves into the same forest, seeking a fresh start — only to find the nightmare never ended. Years down the line, a struggling author obsessed with forgotten folklore follows their stories… unknowingly stepping into a curse older than time itself.
Three stories. One horror.
The deeper they go, the less human they become.

Between Two Worlds is the haunting first volume in a dark fantasy saga rooted in many folklore. Expect ancient magic, forbidden languages, haunted bloodlines — and a war that spans dimensions.

This book is written in Arabic first, then lovingly translated into English by the author. It's a saga meant to shiver down your spine and settle into your dreams.

The story makes us see the world from many characters prespective.
