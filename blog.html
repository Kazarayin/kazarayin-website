I don’t blog. I don’t post often. But sometimes the silence speaks — and this is where I let it echo.

Between Silence and the Veil

May 31, 2025 • by K.A. Zarayin

Stories aren’t born — they break through. Between what I say and what I bury, there are whispers. This space is for them. I won’t promise weekly posts, or even consistency. But when something claws its way out, I’ll leave it here. Maybe someone will find it. Maybe that someone is you.

The First Time I Heard the Jinn

June 1, 2025 • by K.A. Zarayin

It wasn’t a sound. Not really. It was silence bending around the wrong corner of my mind — like something was speaking *between* thoughts. I wasn’t afraid. Not yet. But I stopped writing for a week after that.

I didn’t know it at the time, but that moment would become the seed for what is now Between Two Worlds. You might call it imagination. Or inspiration. I call it a visitor.
