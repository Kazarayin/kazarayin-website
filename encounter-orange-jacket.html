The Guy with The Orange Jacket

I was only 12, home alone for the first time. It was quiet — too quiet — the kind that makes you feel like you're being watched.

I remember hearing footsteps in the hallway, slow, deliberate, dragging. But when I checked, there was no one. I locked every door and tried to convince myself it was nothing.

But the sound came again… closer this time. Right outside my room. I stood frozen, staring at the door — until I saw a shadow move across the bottom. It stopped. And then… it whispered my name.

I didn’t open that door. I didn’t move. I stayed like that until morning light leaked through the window. And even then, I wasn’t sure it was safe.

I never saw it. But I’ve heard it every now and then. And I know it remembers me.
